**Tools:** Illustrator · **Medium:** CAD, technical illustration

Technical flats are the translation layer between a design concept and a production team. They communicate what a sketch cannot: exact seam placement, topstitching, hardware positioning, closure type, pocket construction. Every line is a decision.

These flats were developed for Flats CAD coursework.
