Traveler began with a suitcase and a question: what do you keep when you have to leave everything behind?

Designed during a period of personal transition. Moving cities, living without a fixed address, existing between places. This collection explores modular dressing as a philosophy rather than a gimmick. Each piece is designed to pack flat, layer intuitively, and shift between contexts. A dinner, a flight, a meeting, a morning walk in an unfamiliar city.

The woman this collection is for knows how to travel light without looking like it. She moves through the world with intention. Her wardrobe doesn't announce itself.
