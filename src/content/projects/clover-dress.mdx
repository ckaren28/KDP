**Material:** Green organza · **Technique:** Draping, construction · **Finish:** French seams throughout

The four oversized side pockets are the design origin of this piece — each one a rounded lobe, referencing the four-leaf clover. Organza is unforgiving: every seam decision is visible and permanent. French seaming throughout means every raw edge is enclosed, even in a fabric that catches every stitch.

The tea-length silhouette is shaped entirely by princess seams, no darts. The result is a dress that holds its form while moving like it doesn't.
