## Overview

This collection explores the intersection of luxury evening wear and sustainable fashion practices. Each piece was designed using zero-waste pattern cutting methods and constructed with deadstock fabrics.

## Design Process

- Initial sketches and mood boards
- Pattern development and draping
- Sample construction
- Final garment production

## Materials

- Organic silk charmeuse
- Recycled polyester taff
